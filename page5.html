Project Brief

It is a medical case review management system that is supposed to serve as an EMR for all 15 of the client's locations in and out of the US. The client spent close to $2 million over a period of 2 years and got a system that deployed to only 1 location and crashes almost daily. The monthly cloud service cost was over $10,000 for just 1 location. The client is frustrated.  

Within 2 months, our team tuned up and released the existing system to support all client's locations, and cut down the monthly cloud service cost by 90%.

After that, our team worked on restructuring and migrating the system to a micro-service architecture. So far, the system is operating at all of client's locations with 99.9% availability and the monthly cloud service cost was further reduced to $300 per location.
